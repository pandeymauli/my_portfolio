1. PROBLEM

Redesigning a stable, user friendly and scalable application to ensure smooth business renewals of Adobe's legacy products.


2. THE PRODUCT

RTS or “renewals tracking system” is a desktop application redesigned which enables the in-house team to create and manage quotes for license agreements that are due for renewal.
