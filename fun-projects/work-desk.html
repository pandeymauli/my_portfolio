Personal Work Desk

I needed a desk in my room, and thought of building one. The project took close to 35 hours to complete. I came up with a design which would provide basic storage for books and stationary, and allow me to stash away the wires in a shelf at the back. Since I had used cheap wood (lesson learned!), the finished project didn't look great. So I decided to paint it, and I am very happy with the results.
